Paint with your finger on the screen.

Change Width of Stroke: Tap the brush button and use the slider that is shown to set the desired width. Alternatively you can use the volume control buttons.

Change Transparency of Stroke: Tap the transparency button and use the slider that is shown to set the desired transparency.

Select Color: Tap palette button to bring up the color palette. Touch it and move your finger around until you've got the desired color. You can also pick a color from your painting by touching the painting instead of the palette (the palette is immediately hidden) and move your finger around until you've got the desired color-

Undo Strokes: Press the back button or press the menu button and tap Undo.

Menu Options

New: Clear screen and start a new picture. You may choose a plain background color, select an existing image to draw on or take a picture with the camera and draw on that.

Save: Save your image to the Pictures folder on your SD card. The images are named fingercolors including date and time of saving, e.g. fingercolors-110218-202901.png.

Share: Share your image on Facebook, Picasa, Gmail, ...

Undo: Undo strokes.

Redo: Redo strokes previously un-done.

Help: Show this page.
