Paint with your finger on the screen.

Change Width of Stroke using the volume control buttons (not while this window is open).

Change Opacity of Stroke by moving the trackball left and right.

Select Color by pressing the trackball to bring up the color palette. Touch and move your finger around until you've got the desired color.

Change Brightness of Color by moving the trackball up and down.

Undo Strokes using the back-button.

Press Menu Button for further functionality like New, Save, ...
