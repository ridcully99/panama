Paint with your finger on the screen.

Change Width of Stroke using the volume control buttons.

Change Transparency of Stroke by moving the trackball left and right.

Select Color by pressing the trackball to show the color palette. Touch it and move your finger around until you've got the desired color.

Change Brightness of Color by moving the trackball up and down.

Undo Strokes using the back button.

Menu Options

New: Clear screen and start a new picture. You may choose a plain background color or select an image to draw on.

Save: Save your image to the Pictures folder on your SD card. The images are named fingercolors including date and time of saving, e.g. fingercolors-110218-202901.png.

Redo: Redo strokes previously un-done.
